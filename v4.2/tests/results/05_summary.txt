Key Findings:

3. V=128 pair-seq matches V=64 drive at ~3x budget
4. Network self-limits connections (~7.5% density at V=128)
5. Score still climbing at 48k — no hard wall yet
6. The approach SCALES: bigger V just needs more iterations
