# Legal

## License position

VRAXION is released under the **VRAXION Community Source License 1.0**.

This is a **source-available community license**, not an OSI-approved open source license.

Why: OSI open source licenses must allow commercial use and free redistribution without royalty restrictions. VRAXION intentionally allows broad free community use while requiring royalty payments for monetized third-party access to VRAXION-powered functionality.

## Free community use

No royalty is owed for:

- personal use;
- research;
- education;
- nonprofit use;
- community forks;
- free public demos with no monetization;
- self-hosting;
- internal company use where third parties are not charged for access to VRAXION-powered functionality.

## Royalty use

Royalty is owed when a person or entity monetizes third-party access to VRAXION-powered functionality.

Examples:

- paid hosted service;
- paid API;
- paid SaaS;
- paid subscription;
- paid managed deployment;
- paid enterprise access;
- paid app where VRAXION-powered functionality is a material feature;
- ad-supported, sponsored, white-label, reseller, or platform-bundled access.

Royalty: **19% of Attributable Net Revenue**.

Default allocation:

- **1% Founder Allocation**
- **18% VRAXION Forever Prize Allocation**

After a Founder Redirect Event, the Founder Allocation redirects to the Prize and the full **19%** goes to the **VRAXION Forever Prize Allocation**.

The total royalty is always **19%**, not 20%.

## Net Revenue definition

Net Revenue means revenue from Royalty Use minus only:

- refunds;
- chargebacks;
- VAT / sales tax / GST remitted to a tax authority;
- payment processor fees;
- app-store or platform distribution fees capped at 30%;
- user-facing discounts applied at sale.

Not deductible:

- cloud compute;
- hosting;
- salaries;
- R&D;
- training data;
- model licenses;
- marketing;
- office costs;
- legal costs;
- affiliate fees;
- internal transfer pricing;
- general overhead.
