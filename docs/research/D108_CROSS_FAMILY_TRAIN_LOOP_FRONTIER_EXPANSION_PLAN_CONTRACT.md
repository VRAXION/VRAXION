# D108 Cross-Family Train Loop Frontier Expansion Plan Contract

## Purpose
D108 is the controlled symbolic frontier-expansion planning milestone after D107 scale confirmation. It decides the safe D109 direction by auditing Lane A preservation, Lane B mixed-family normalization, Lane C trig-periodic targeted repair readiness, Lane D expansion-family safety, and Lane E symbolic sequence bridge readiness.

## Boundary
D108 is non-destructive dry-run planning only. It does not run full cross-family expansion training, does not perform natural-language pretraining, does not train a Gemma-class model, does not use raw visual Raven tasks, and does not increase sparsity above 8%.

## Upstream audit
The runner must validate or restore/rerun the D107 handoff and write `d107_upstream_manifest.json` with the requested D107 commit, commit/artifact presence, restore status, replayed decision, replayed next task, replayed D108 readiness, replayed Lane A/B/C status, sparse identity replay, failed jobs, and observed push status.

## Frontier expansion lanes
- Lane A: the 12 D107 passing families remain the protected preservation anchor.
- Lane B: `MIXED_SYMBOLIC_TRANSFER_FAMILY` is evaluated for promotion from guarded partial to provisional-normal training with guarded stop gates.
- Lane C: `TRIG_PERIODIC_SYMBOLIC_FAMILY` is evaluated for targeted repair readiness and remains outside the healthy claim.
- Lane D: eight new controlled symbolic expansion families are safety-mapped without contaminating Lane A.
- Lane E: symbolic sequence and language-like symbolic command families are bridge candidates only; they are not natural-language training.

## D109 recommendation policy
D108 may recommend `D109_FRONTIER_EXPANSION_TRAIN_LOOP_PROTOTYPE` only when Lane A preservation is safe, Lane B status is explicitly decided, Lane C targeted repair remains positive, at least three Lane D expansion families pass safety gates, D109 objective/batch/curriculum/rollback/eval/checkpoint/metric gates are defined, sparse identity is preserved, leak sentinels collapse, Rust path remains invoked, fallback rows are zero, and failed jobs are empty.

## Frozen identity
The symbolic solver, dense baseline, protected symbolic router, protected components, and 8% sparse mask remain frozen by default. D109 is recommended as adapter-only unless separately approved.
