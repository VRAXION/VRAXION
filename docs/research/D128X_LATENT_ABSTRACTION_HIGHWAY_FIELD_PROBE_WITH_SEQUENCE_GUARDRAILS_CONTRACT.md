# D128X Latent Abstraction Highway Field Probe with Sequence Guardrails Contract

Purpose: run a diagnostic/shadow-only latent abstraction highway field probe after D127 to test whether controlled symbolic routing improves when it selects the highest safe abstraction level and lowest safe resistance path instead of walking every local step.

Boundary: D128X is a controlled symbolic diagnostic probe only. It performs no mainline training, no adapter mutation, no dataset mutation, no natural-language pretraining, introduces no tokenizer or next-token objective, uses no raw text corpus or raw Raven tasks, and does not train a Gemma-class model.

Upstream: D127 must replay or restore as d127_adversarial_template_repair_scale_confirmed_gated_branch with d128_ready=true, selected_branch=gated_multi_correction, gated_branch_wins=true, adversarial/template/grammar/route reductions intact, preservation intact, sparse identity intact, fallback_rows=0, and failed_jobs=[].

Probe fields: D128X evaluates abstraction_force, detail_disambiguation_force, goal_route_flow_force, and verification_brake_force through resistance components for energy, local steps, abstraction jumps, landing risk, shortcut risk, route uncertainty, counterfactual fragility, preservation risk, calibration mismatch, and verification cost.

Probe arms: D128X compares local step-by-step, max abstraction, shortest jump, lowest energy, lowest safe resistance, highest safe abstraction, gated correction plus resistance, random abstraction, and oracle-reference-only arms without using forbidden abstraction/family/case/branch/before-after/scale labels as predictive shortcuts.

Family policy: long-sequence, nested, adversarial template/grammar, same/different surface, and binding-shadow families are diagnostic-only in D128X; guarded and reference-only policies from D127 are preserved and no family is promoted into a full healthy claim.

Positive gate: D128X passes only if D127 validates or restores, scale is not reduced, all reports emit, abstraction highway diagnostics execute, no training or mutation occurs, safe-resistance/highest-safe/gated-resistance arms and controls are evaluated, preservation and leak/sentinel reports pass, fallback_rows=0, failed_jobs=[], and report/metric crosschecks pass.

Decision target: d128x_gated_resistance_field_probe_positive -> D128_CONTROLLED_SYMBOLIC_BRIDGE_FRONTIER_CONSOLIDATION_PLAN_WITH_GATED_RESISTANCE_FIELD_NOTE when gated correction plus resistance is strongest and all gates pass.
