# D114 Symbolic Sequence Bridge Prototype With Trig Guardrails Contract

D114 executes the first limited, checkpointed, adapter-only controlled symbolic-sequence bridge prototype using the D113 plan and D112 trig guardrails. It may update only bridge adapter deltas for ready and guarded symbolic bridge candidates while preserving Lane A, Lane B, Lane C trig repair-only guardrails, Lane D, sparse identity, top1 guard, D68, halting/convergence, Rust path, and leak/shortcut safety.

## Boundary

D114 does not perform natural-language pretraining, does not introduce a tokenizer, does not define next-token prediction, does not use raw text corpora, does not use raw Raven, does not train a Gemma-class model, and does not make AGI/consciousness/production-readiness claims. The symbolic solver, dense baseline, protected symbolic router, protected components, and D102 8% light sparse mask remain frozen.

## Trainable and held bridge families

Trainable guarded bridge candidates are `SYMBOLIC_SEQUENCE_ROUTING_FAMILY` and `ORDERED_RULE_CHAIN_SYMBOLIC_FAMILY`. Guarded low-weight candidates are `LANGUAGE_LIKE_SYMBOLIC_COMMAND_FAMILY`, `SYMBOLIC_COMMAND_COMPOSITION_FAMILY`, and `VARIABLE_BINDING_SEQUENCE_FAMILY`; the language-like family remains symbolic command-routing only. `MULTI_STEP_INSTRUCTION_ROUTING_FAMILY` is reference-only and excluded from the healthy training claim.

## Positive gate

D114 is confirmed only if D113 replay is valid, scale is not reduced, all boundary constraints hold, sparse/protected identity is preserved, adapter-only bridge training executes with checkpoints and no rollback, all trainable and guarded bridge family gates pass, trig remains repair-only, Lane A/B/D preservation holds, leak/shortcut sentinels pass, Rust path is invoked, fallback rows and failed jobs are zero, and reports are consistent.
