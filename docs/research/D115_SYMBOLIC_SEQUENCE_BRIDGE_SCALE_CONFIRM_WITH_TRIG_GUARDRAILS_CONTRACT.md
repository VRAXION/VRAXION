# D115 Symbolic Sequence Bridge Scale Confirm With Trig Guardrails Contract

D115 scale-confirms the D114 adapter-only controlled symbolic-sequence bridge prototype with trig guardrails. It checks that the bridge remains stable at larger scale while preserving Lane A core families, Lane B provisional mixed status, Lane C trig repair-only guardrails, Lane D frontier expansion families, sparse identity, D68, top1 guard, Rust path, and leak/shortcut safety.

## Boundary

D115 is not a new bridge design and does not perform natural-language pretraining, tokenizer work, next-token prediction, raw text corpus training, raw Raven processing, Gemma-class training, or AGI/consciousness/production-readiness claims. The symbolic solver, dense baseline, protected symbolic router, protected components, and D102 8% light sparse mask remain frozen.

## Bridge families

Trainable guarded scale families are `SYMBOLIC_SEQUENCE_ROUTING_FAMILY` and `ORDERED_RULE_CHAIN_SYMBOLIC_FAMILY`.
