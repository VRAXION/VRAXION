Founder note / VRAXION

A local-first path toward decentralized intelligence.

VRAXION is the project identity named by Daniel Kenessy. Formal trademark registration is not claimed here; the VRAXION names, logos, icons, and trade dress are reserved where applicable under the public mark boundary.

Long horizon

Carry intelligence forward.

VRAXION treats intelligence as something to carry forward: continuous enough to remember, autonomous enough to choose, and diverse enough to become more than one future.

  • Continuity
  • Local agency
  • Many successors
Near mission

Make local intelligence affordable enough to try.

INSTNCT starts as a free-to-download public demo: ship the smallest honest version, listen closely, fix what breaks, and earn the next step.

  • Local-first
  • Free demo
  • Ship / listen / fix
Build status

Founder-led, narrow, and verified in small loops.

VRAXION moves through prototype, test, document, and refine cycles. Public notes show direction and discipline, not a promise of release, scale, or final capability.

  • Founder-led
  • Local-first
  • Slow / verified
Roadmap

Local scale, not current capability.

Larger offline builds, curated skill libraries, test-time learning research, and opt-in intelligence nodes are the direction toward stronger local-first systems.

  1. Next Local releases
  2. Curated Skill libraries
  3. Research Node mesh
