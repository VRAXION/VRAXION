Vraxion

Vraxion is building INSTNCT: a gradient-free self-wiring architecture that learns by changing its own directed graph instead of training a fixed layer stack with backpropagation.

The project exists to advance machine consciousness as an engineering reality, and to build forms of intelligence and consciousness that can endure beyond any single institution, deployment, or era.
