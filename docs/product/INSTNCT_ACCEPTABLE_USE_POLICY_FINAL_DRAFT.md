# INSTNCT Acceptable Use Policy Final Draft

Status: 060 license package draft.

This document is draft only. It is not final legal terms. Counsel review
required before external use. No agreement or permission starts from this draft
alone.

## Allowed Review Uses

- Noncommercial research.
- Reproduction of bounded INSTNCT results.
- Internal evaluation.
- Visual audit and debugging.
- Synthetic-data demos.
- Non-clinical hospital administration exploration.
- Non-high-stakes classroom support.
- Commercial evaluation under written agreement.

## Uses Requiring Written Approval

- Commercial use.
- Customer-data processing.
- Processing health, education, financial, biometric, or other sensitive data.
- Hospital or school pilot candidates.
- Redistribution or embedding into another product.
- Hosted access request, subject to later review.

## Prohibited Without Separate Compliance Review

- Diagnosis, treatment, triage, medication decisions, or clinical decision
  support.
- Grading, admissions, student ranking, placement, discipline, proctoring, or
  high-stakes profiling.
- Social scoring.
- Predictive policing.
- Biometric identification or sensitive-attribute inference.
- Emotion recognition in workplace or education settings.
- Eligibility decisions for essential public or private services.
- Safety-critical autonomous control.
- Claims of consciousness, biological equivalence, or physical quantum
  behavior.

## User Obligations Direction

- Preserve the claim boundary.
- Identify machine-generated outputs where applicable.
- Keep humans responsible for consequential decisions.
- Avoid regulated personal data unless covered by separate written agreement.
- Keep audit logs for evaluations.
- Report safety or misuse incidents.
