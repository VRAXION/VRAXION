# INSTNCT Commercial License Template Draft

Status: 060 license package draft.

This document is draft only. It is not final legal terms. Counsel review
required before external use. No agreement or permission starts from this draft
alone.

## Commercial License Trigger

A separate written commercial license is required for:

- selling INSTNCT-based products or services
- hosted access
- embedding INSTNCT into another product
- paid consulting delivery
- production customer workflows
- redistribution outside noncommercial terms
- internal business production use
- training/evaluating customer systems as a paid service

## Commercial Template Direction

The written commercial agreement should define:

- licensed entity and affiliates
- licensed deployment mode
- permitted use case
- data handling terms
- support tier and support boundary
- audit and logging obligations
- checkpoint and artifact ownership
- update, rollback, and termination terms
- prohibited claim language

## Regulated-Use Carveout

Commercial license alone does not authorize:

- diagnosis
- treatment
- triage
- medication decisions
- clinical decision support or CDS
- grading
- admissions
- student ranking
- placement
- proctoring
- high-stakes profiling

Any such use requires separate compliance review and separate written approval.
