# INSTNCT Public-Benefit Terms Draft

Status: 059 public-benefit terms draft.

This document is draft only. It is not final legal terms. Counsel review is
required before external use. No external pilot may start from this draft alone.

## Draft Terms Direction

- A public-benefit organization may request review for a pilot candidate.
- Written approval is required before any external pilot.
- Use is restricted to non-clinical only or non-high-stakes only scope.
- Deployment is restricted to `local_research` or `private_evaluation`.
- Sensitive data requires separate written agreement and review.
- The organization must name a human owner.
- Audit logs and closeout review are required.
- The pilot may be rejected or stopped for claim-boundary conflict.

## Not Granted By This Draft

- No production deployment right.
- No hosted SaaS access.
- No public beta access.
- No approval for clinical workflows.
- No approval for high-stakes education workflows.
- No approval for PHI or student education records without separate agreement.
