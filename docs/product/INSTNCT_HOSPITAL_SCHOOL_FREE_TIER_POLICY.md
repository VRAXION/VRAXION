# INSTNCT Hospital and School Free-Tier Policy

Status: 056 productization planning artifact.

This document defines the first free-tier boundary for hospitals and schools.
It is a use policy, not a regulatory clearance.

## Hospital Free Tier

Allowed first-scope hospital uses:

- Administrative workflow exploration.
- Research support.
- Internal document routing.
- Model behavior visualization.
- Non-clinical education or simulation.
- Audit/replay of bounded research examples.

Forbidden before separate compliance approval:

- Diagnosis.
- Treatment recommendation.
- Triage.
- Medication decision.
- Clinical decision support.
- Emergency prioritization.
- Patient-specific risk scoring.
- Any output used directly for patient care.

## School Free Tier

Allowed first-scope school uses:

- Tutor/explanation support.
- Practice generation.
- Classroom experiments.
- Teacher-support dashboard.
- Non-high-stakes learning aid.
- Research or demonstration use.

Forbidden before separate compliance approval:

- Grading.
- Admissions.
- Student ranking.
- High-stakes profiling.
- Discipline decisions.
- Test proctoring or prohibited-behavior detection.
- Placement decisions.

## Required Free-Tier Controls

- Written use-case description.
- Human oversight.
- No autonomous decision authority.
- No clinical or high-stakes education claims.
- Data minimization.
- No upload of regulated personal data unless a separate data agreement exists.
- Disable/rollback path.
