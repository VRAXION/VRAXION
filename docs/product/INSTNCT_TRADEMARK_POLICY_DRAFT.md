# INSTNCT Trademark Policy Draft

Status: 060 license package draft.

This document is draft only. It is not final legal terms. Counsel review
required before external use. No agreement or permission starts from this draft
alone.

## Trademark Direction

The INSTNCT and VRAXION marks should identify the official project and approved
offerings. Trademark permission is separate from source or commercial license
permission.

## Forbidden Trademark Implications

Do not use INSTNCT or VRAXION marks to imply:

- endorsement
- official partnership
- production readiness
- clinical readiness
- high-stakes education readiness
- full VRAXION
- language grounding
- consciousness

## Fork And Integration Direction

Forks, integrations, consulting packages, and hosted services must not imply
official status, supported production release, clinical suitability, or
high-stakes education suitability without separate written trademark
permission.
