# INSTNCT Acceptable Use Policy

Status: 056 productization planning artifact.

This document defines an initial acceptable-use boundary for research,
evaluation, commercial licensing, and public-benefit pilots.

## Allowed Uses

Allowed within current research/productization boundary:

- Noncommercial research.
- Reproduction of bounded INSTNCT results.
- Internal evaluation.
- Visual audit and debugging.
- Synthetic-data demos.
- Non-clinical hospital administration exploration.
- Non-high-stakes classroom support.
- Commercial evaluation under written agreement.

## Uses Requiring Written Approval

- Commercial use.
- Hosted access.
- Customer-data processing.
- Processing health, education, financial, biometric, or other sensitive data.
- Hospital or school pilots.
- Redistribution or embedding into another product.

## Prohibited Without Separate Compliance Approval

- Clinical diagnosis, treatment, triage, medication, or clinical decision
  support.
- Grading, admissions, student ranking, placement, discipline, or proctoring.
- Social scoring.
- Predictive policing.
- Biometric identification or sensitive-attribute inference.
- Emotion recognition in workplace or education settings.
- Eligibility decisions for essential public or private services.
- Safety-critical autonomous control.
- Claims of consciousness, biological equivalence, or physical quantum behavior.

## User Obligations

Users must:

- Preserve the claim boundary.
- Identify when outputs are machine-generated.
- Keep humans responsible for consequential decisions.
- Avoid uploading regulated personal data unless covered by a written agreement.
- Keep audit logs for evaluations.
- Report safety or misuse incidents.

## Claim Boundary

056 supports productization planning only. It does not support production
release, public beta promotion, production API readiness, clinical readiness,
school high-stakes readiness, full VRAXION, language grounding, consciousness,
biological/FlyWire equivalence, or physical quantum behavior.
