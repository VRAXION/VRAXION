# INSTNCT Source-Available Noncommercial License Draft

Status: 060 license package draft.

This document is draft only. It is not final legal terms. Counsel review
required before external use. No agreement or permission starts from this draft
alone.

## License Position

INSTNCT is intended to be source-available/noncommercial for research,
evaluation, and approved public-interest work. Commercial use requires a
separate written commercial license.

This package is source-available, not open source. It is not OSI open source.
The reason is simple: commercial restrictions and field-of-use limits do not
fit OSI criteria.

## Draft Grant Direction

- Noncommercial research and evaluation may be allowed under public
  source-available terms.
- Public-interest use may be allowed only inside the public-benefit rider and
  pilot boundary.
- Commercial use requires separate written commercial license.
- Redistribution outside the noncommercial terms requires separate written
  permission.
- No clinical, high-stakes education, production deployment, hosted SaaS, or
  public beta rights are granted by this draft.

## Required Notice Direction

Downstream recipients should preserve license notices, claim-boundary notices,
and acceptable-use notices. Exact required notice language remains counsel
review material.
