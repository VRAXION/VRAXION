# INSTNCT Commercial License Boundary

Status: 056 productization planning artifact.

This document defines what should require a separate written commercial license.
It is not final license text.

## Commercial Use Triggers

A separate commercial license is required for:

- Selling INSTNCT-based products or services.
- Using INSTNCT in revenue-generating customer workflows.
- Offering hosted access to INSTNCT.
- Embedding INSTNCT into commercial software.
- Paid consulting delivery using INSTNCT as a material component.
- Training, fine-tuning, or evaluating customer systems as a paid service.
- Redistributing modified source or binaries outside the noncommercial terms.

## Commercial Evaluation

Commercial entities may need a separate evaluation agreement even before
production use.

Evaluation agreement should cover:

- Scope.
- Duration.
- Data handling.
- Confidentiality.
- No production use.
- No regulated-use claims.
- Audit artifact ownership.

## Revenue-Share / Royalty Term Inputs

056 does not set a default commercial rate. Future agreements may choose:

- Fixed annual license.
- Seat-based license.
- Usage-based license.
- Enterprise site license.
- Revenue-share.
- Royalty on INSTNCT-derived product revenue.
- Support and integration retainer.

## Restricted Commercial Uses

Even with a commercial license, the following require separate compliance
approval:

- Healthcare diagnosis, treatment, triage, medication, or clinical decision
  support.
- Education grading, admissions, student ranking, or high-stakes profiling.
- Biometric identification, emotion recognition, social scoring, predictive
  policing, or essential-service eligibility decisions.
- Safety-critical control systems.

## Claim Boundary

056 supports productization planning only. It does not support production
release, public beta promotion, production API readiness, clinical readiness,
school high-stakes readiness, full VRAXION, language grounding, consciousness,
biological/FlyWire equivalence, or physical quantum behavior.
